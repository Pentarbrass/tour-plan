What people think about us

Megan Fox

Stayed 18 Nov, 2019

It was very nice hotel with cleanliness. Staff behavior was good and polite. They welcome us very well. Issue was only that Lift was not in working and we were allotted to 3rd floor and amenities articles were in corner of gallery which were giving bad feeling. Breakfast was good and support of the staff was also very nice. Location is not good as per atmosphere, it is very nearby most of the popular places but self location in a narrow street is not good. Overall it was a good experience and could recommend.

Last minute booking when planner didn’t secure our hotel. Frantically the day before I went online found this Hilton and just hoped for the best. It was better than I expected. Uma Thurman

Stayed 20 Dec, 2018

The hotel was very good, infact great. Pros: 1. Hotel service was great. You can order food or any other service from the app they have provided. It was so convenient. 2. The price was cheaper than other similar hotels. Con: 1. Bathroom of the room had a window that opens up to the lobby which was very uncomfortable. there was no way to close the window. Ours was on 2nd floor so it was not that big an issue I would have worried more if it would have been on ground floor. 2. Restaurant services were poor. Pool bar was worst. They were probably understaffed. I had to ask for water 3 times in half an hour to get water. In other restaurants too the service was very slow.
